Our story

One mixer. One oven. A whole lot of butter.

The Mad Batter started the way most late-night dessert cravings do — with a recipe that needed one more try, and a partner who politely said "you should just sell these." So here we are.

We run a small home kitchen in Prince George, BC, baking everything fresh once your order is in. No mass production, no shortcuts, no fondant that tastes like plastic. Just the real-deal stuff.

How to order

It's a three-step kind of thing.

  1. Send the deets. Fill out the order form below — what you want, when, and for how many people.
  2. We confirm. We'll reply with availability, a final price, and pickup details in Prince George.
  3. Pickup day. We bake it fresh and meet you at the agreed time. You take it home and look like a hero.

Heads up: most orders need at least 48 hours. Big custom cakes — give us a week if you can.
