FAQ

Quick answers

How far in advance should I order?

Most items need at least 48 hours. Fully custom cakes (especially tiered or decorated) do best with a week's notice. Holiday weekends — even earlier.

Do you deliver?

Pickup only right now, in Prince George. The pickup address is shared when your order is confirmed.

Can you do allergen-friendly or dietary requests?

Ask! We're a small kitchen so we can't guarantee a fully allergen-free environment, but we can usually work with gluten-free, nut-free, or vegan requests on most items.

Do I need to pay a deposit?

For custom cakes, yes — usually 50% to lock in the date. Smaller items (cookies, bars, dozen cupcakes) can be paid at pickup.

What's your most popular order?

Custom cakes for birthdays and events, and the filled cupcakes. The 9" round chocolate cake is a forever favorite.

Can I send a screenshot of a cake I want copied?

Absolutely — bring a reference photo to your order and we'll quote it. We don't do exact replicas of other bakers' work, but we love a "vibe" to start from.
