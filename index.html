Our story

Baked in a small kitchen. Shared with a big city.

The Mad Batter started the way most good things do — with a mixing bowl, a too-hot oven, and a recipe that needed one more try. Today it's a family-run bakery in Prince George, BC, turning out the kind of treats that make the whole block smell like butter and sugar.

We don't do mass production. We do small batches, seasonal flavors, and the brownies your friend keeps telling you about.
